Our Story

SBT X is a strategic outsourcing and recruitment partner headquartered in the United Arab Emirates, supporting technology-driven and high-growth IT companies for over 18 years. Across multiple regions, SBT X integrates talent delivery with a full operational backbone – covering recruitment, HR, payroll, accounting support, legal, compliance, administrative, and technical delivery – within one coordinated system designed to eliminate vendor fragmentation and ensure clarity, control, and dependable execution.

This model has supported more than 2,300 professionals across international markets, including 100+ senior specialist and executive-level placements. Our experience spans both scale-ups and large global enterprises, consistently delivering results in complex, compliance-driven environments. We are recognized by major industry associations for reliability and regulatory precision, including top-tier positioning within established IT BPO industry benchmarks and membership in leading business networks – underscoring our consistency and long-term client trust.

Our team combines strong professional and academic backgrounds, shaped by over 30 years of cumulative executive leadership experience, including C-suite and senior management roles across finance, operations, and technology-driven businesses. SBT X was founded by professionals who have led companies themselves and understand execution from the client’s side – from board-level decision-making to hands-on operational responsibility. This perspective has shaped a culture grounded in accountability, structure, and delivery discipline, with each engagement managed to the rigor of an internal function and focused on translating strategy into clear, tangible results.

Integrated Multi-Function Delivery Model

SBT X operates as a single execution partner, eliminating the need to coordinate multiple vendors across talent, payroll, legal, compliance, and IT. By consolidating accountability within one operating model, we reduce handover risk, shorten decision cycles, and ensure consistent governance across jurisdictions. This integrated structure enables companies to scale teams and operations with clarity and control, delivering predictable execution even in complex, multi-market environments – while providing leadership with a single point of ownership and full operational visibility from hiring through day-to-day execution.

Elite Human Capital Across Technical & Executive Domains

SBT X delivers integrated human capital, uniting deep technical execution with senior cross-functional leadership. Drawing from high-skill European engineering hubs, our talent includes specialists in AI, cloud, cybersecurity, and software engineering, operating in complex, production-grade environments. Beyond technology, we provide senior financial controllers, legal and compliance specialists, and executive consultants with multinational leadership experience. Embedded directly into client operations, they bring disciplined judgment and operational authority – strengthening governance, sharpening alignment, and ensuring decisions are executed properly, not deferred or diluted.

Enterprise-Grade Partnerships & Standards

SBT X maintains long-standing relationships with major international technology firms and high-growth ventures, refining our ability to meet enterprise governance, data-security, and regulatory standards. We operate with the discipline and accountability expected inside multinational organizations, aligning our delivery to each client’s internal policies and reporting frameworks. Our operating model is designed to integrate cleanly into existing enterprise environments. This enterprise-ready capability makes SBT X a trusted extension of leadership teams – providing execution stability, transparent oversight, and operational excellence.
