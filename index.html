About Us

SBT X is a UAE-based strategic outsourcing and recruitment partner for technology-driven and high-growth companies. We operate through a unified delivery model that integrates elite talent with a comprehensive operational backbone under a single point of ownership, enabling us to act as one execution partner. This structure eliminates vendor fragmentation, consolidates accountability, and provides leadership with clear governance and operational visibility.

Delivery is led by senior professionals embedded directly within client operations, drawing on deep experience supporting complex, international technology environments. Founded by former operators with decades of senior executive leadership experience, SBT X manages each engagement with internal-level rigor – operating as an extension of the client’s organization and focused on delivering tangible, measurable results.

Our Operating Philosophy

SBT X was built around a practical observation: growth rarely fails at the level of intent – it fails in the details most organizations underestimate. As scale increases, small inefficiencies compound, coordination slows, and both people and processes can drift out of alignment.

We focus deliberately on these pressure points. By applying disciplined judgment to how capability is introduced, embedded, and supported – and to how responsibility and coordination are structured around it – we help organizations operate reliably under conditions where complexity would otherwise erode performance.
